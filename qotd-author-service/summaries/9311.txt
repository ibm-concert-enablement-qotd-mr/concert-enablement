Michelle Christine Trachtenberg (; born October 11, 1985) is an American actress and model. Starting her career at a young age, she has played in a number of commercials, films, and television series. She is best known for playing Jennifer in EuroTrip (2004), Nona Mecklenberg in The Adventures of Pete & Pete (1994–1996), Dawn Summers in Buffy the Vampire Slayer (2000–2003), Harriet M. Welsch in Harriet the Spy (1996), and her recurring role as Georgina Sparks in Gossip Girl (2007–2012).
